Why can I help

Many families need you. They are living in tents and informal settlements, struggling to afford their basic winter needs. The cold and brutal weather makes the hardship of forced displacement even worse. Our team continues to support families in these difficult circumstances, providing warm clothes to those in need. Please help us to keep these families safe and warm. Donate Here

How can I help

Many people ask this question or even they think they can't help. We can help by any piece of fabric even a little scarf. If you can't buy new clothes, denote some of your clothes to make a little baby warm or even any poor person. Give your clothes to someone who needs and take a little smile that makes your day.
